Lemon Water

Description

Enjoy your refreshing lemon water! It's perfect for hydration and a boost of vitamin C.

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon
  • 1 glass of water (about 8 oz)
  • Ice (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the lemon in half.
  2. Squeeze the juice from one half into a glass of water.
  3. Stir well and add ice if you prefer it cold.
  4. Optionally, garnish with a few lemon slices or mint leaves.

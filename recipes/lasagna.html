Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe features layers of rich, savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta filling, and gooey melted mozzarella, all nestled between tender sheets of pasta. It’s a comforting and hearty dish perfect for any occasion, from casual dinners to family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 450g of ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup red wine (optional but adds depth)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (to balance acidity)
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional for a little heat)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 425g of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese (divided)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder (optional)
  • 12 lasagna noodles (regular or no-boil)
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish, optional)

Steps

Cook the Meat Sauce

  1. In a large skillet or saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add the ground beef (or meat of your choice), breaking it apart with a spoon. Cook until browned, about 8-10 minutes.
  5. Once browned, drain any excess fat if necessary.
  6. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and red wine (if using). Stir to combine.
  7. Add the sugar, basil, oregano, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Stir and let the sauce simmer on low heat for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, to allow the flavors to meld. Adjust seasoning to taste.

Prepare the Ricotta Mixture

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, parsley, and garlic powder (if using).
  2. Mix until smooth and set aside.

Cook the Lasagna Noodles

  1. If using regular lasagna noodles, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the noodles and cook according to the package instructions (typically 8-10 minutes), until al dente.
  3. Drain the noodles and rinse them with cold water to prevent sticking.
  4. If using no-boil noodles, you can skip this step.

Assemble the Lasagna

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Lay down the first layer of lasagna noodles (3-4 noodles depending on the size of your dish).
  4. Add a layer of ricotta mixture on top of the noodles (about 1/4 of the mixture).
  5. Spoon over a generous layer of meat sauce (about 1/4 of the sauce).
  6. Sprinkle with 1/3 cup of mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat the layers (noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, mozzarella) two more times.
  8. For the final layer, top with a layer of noodles, the remaining meat sauce, and the remaining mozzarella cheese.
  9. Sprinkle the top with a little Parmesan cheese for extra flavor.

Bake the Lasagna

  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, making sure it doesn't touch the cheese.
  2. Bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and slightly golden on top.
  3. Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing, as it helps the layers set.

Serve the Lasagna

  1. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.
  2. Slice and serve with a side of garlic bread or a salad!

Italian Soda

Description

That's it! You've got yourself a refreshing, fizzy Italian soda.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of club soda (or sparkling water)
  • 2â€“3 tablespoons of flavored syrup (e.g., raspberry, cherry, or strawberry)
  • Ice
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Fill a glass with ice.
  2. Pour the flavored syrup over the ice.
  3. Slowly add the club soda and stir gently to mix.
  4. Top with whipped cream if you want an extra indulgent touch.
